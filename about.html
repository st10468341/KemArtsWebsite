About KEM Arts

KEM Arts is a growing creative venture dedicated to producing and sharing unique, high-quality artworks with a wider audience. This business was established as a platform to help me, a young and passionate artist, not only sell my work but also build the foundation for my future as a professional within the creative industry. Through this initiative, I am able to sustain my practice by investing in the tools and supplies I need, while also using the income to support further studies and personal development. KEM Arts primarily shares new work on Instagram, at local coffee shop pop-up events, and occasionally through exhibitions, giving both locals and online audiences an opportunity to experience my art directly.

The Artist

My name is Kyle-Ethan Meiring, and I am the founder and artist behind KEM Arts. Born on 14 April 2007 in South Africa, I am currently 18 years old and have been immersed in art since a very young age. I began formal art training at just 8 years old under the guidance of Braam Coetsee, whose mentorship has been instrumental in helping me develop both technical skills and creative discipline. Over the years, my passion for art has only deepened, and today I see it as more than just a hobbyâ€”it is a calling and the career I aspire to build for myself. KEM Arts represents not only my personal journey but also my commitment to sharing creativity and meaningful visual experiences with others.

My Art Style

My work primarily explores the use of acrylic paints, often applied with palette knives to create distinct layers of texture and depth. This method allows me to capture a sense of movement and energy on the canvas, giving each piece its own identity and character. I aim to create art that speaks both visually and emotionally, drawing inspiration from everyday life, my environment, and the people I encounter. The tactile, textured quality of my paintings encourages viewers to experience art not only through colour and form but also through the surface and structure of the work itself. Each painting is a reflection of my personal growth, experiments with technique, and dedication to my craft.

Our Mission

The mission of KEM Arts is to make art approachable, meaningful, and accessible to everyone. I strive to create small collections of work that are affordable yet carry a strong sense of individuality and authenticity. By sharing my art through pop-ups and exhibitions, I create opportunities to meet and engage with people who are genuinely interested in the story and effort behind each piece. At the same time, KEM Arts serves as a way for me to invest in my education, purchase essential materials, and sustain the creative practice that I hope to continue for many years. The ultimate goal is to grow not only as an individual artist but also as part of a broader community that values the power and importance of creative expression.

Exhibitions

KEM Arts has had the privilege of displaying artworks in a variety of local settings, including coffee shops, markets, and community events. These exhibitions provide valuable opportunities for me to share my work face-to-face with art lovers and collectors, offering a more personal connection that cannot always be achieved online. Being based in the Western Province of South Africa, I take pride in contributing to my local creative scene by making art visible and accessible in everyday community spaces. In the future, I look forward to expanding these opportunities and exploring new venues where my work can reach a wider audience.
