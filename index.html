Welcome to KEM Arts

KEM Arts is a South African art business created by young artist Kyle-Ethan Meiring. The goal of KEM Arts is to share original artworks with people both online and in the local community. Each piece is made with passion, using acrylic paints and palette knives to create textured and expressive art.

About the Artist

My name is Kyle-Ethan Meiring, I am 18 years old, and I was born on 14 April 2007. I have been learning and practicing art since I was 8 years old under the guidance of Braam Coetsee. Art has always been my passion, and I created KEM Arts to help me grow as a young artist while also supporting my studies and buying more art supplies. Through my work, I hope to inspire others and bring colour and creativity into their spaces.

What We Offer

KEM Arts shares new artwork through Instagram, coffee shop pop-up displays, and local exhibitions. Each piece is unique and made with care. Small collections are released to keep the work affordable and special. Commissions are also available for those who want something personal and unique.
