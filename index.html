Welcome to KEM Arts

KEM Arts is a South African art business created by young artist Kyle-Ethan Meiring. Each piece is made with passion using acrylic paints and palette knives to create textured and expressive art.

About the Artist

My name is Kyle-Ethan Meiring, I am 18 years old, and I was born on 14 April 2007. I have been practicing art since I was 8 under the guidance of Braam Coetsee. Through my work, I hope to inspire others and bring colour and creativity into their spaces.

What We Offer

Original Artworks

Discover unique pieces created with acrylic paints and palette knives. Each artwork is expressive, textured, and made with passion.

Local Exhibitions

Experience KEM Arts in local coffee shops and exhibitions. Small collections are released to keep the work affordable and special.
